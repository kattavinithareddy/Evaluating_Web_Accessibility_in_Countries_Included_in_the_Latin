{% if policy.framework_type == 'wcag' %}
  • Provide text alternatives for non-text content
  • Ensure content is keyboard accessible
  • Use sufficient color contrast ratios
  • Make navigation consistent and predictable
  • Provide clear error messages and suggestions
  • Ensure compatibility with assistive technologies
{% elif policy.framework_type == 'section_508' %}

{% elif policy.framework_type == 'ada' %}

{% elif policy.framework_type == 'en_301_549' %}

  • Covers mobile applications and software
  • Includes hardware and documentation requirements

  • Follow international accessibility standards
  • Ensure digital content is accessible to all users

✅ Compliance Tips

  • Conduct regular automated and manual accessibility testing
  • Include users with disabilities in testing processes
  • Maintain an accessibility statement on your website
  • Provide multiple ways to contact for accessibility issues
  • Document remediation efforts and timelines
  • Train content creators and developers on accessibility
  • Use this platform to scan and monitor your projects
